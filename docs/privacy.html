CoFrame 隐私政策

最后更新：2026-04-26

CoFrame 不收集、不上传你的任何数据。 所有录制文件仅保存在你设备本地的 App 沙盒（草稿箱）中， 是否导出到系统相册完全由你主动选择。

不收集任何数据

CoFrame 不会：

  • 不上传任何视频、音频或元数据到任何服务器
  • 不接入任何第三方分析、广告、统计或追踪 SDK
  • 不要求账号注册，不收集你的姓名、邮箱、手机号、设备 ID
  • 不读取你已有的照片、视频、通讯录或任何系统数据

访问的系统权限

📷 相机 (Camera)
用于横屏 + 竖屏视频录制。仅在你打开 App 时使用，不会后台访问。
🎙 麦克风 (Microphone)
用于为录制视频采集声音。仅在你打开 App 时使用，不会后台录音。
🖼 相册 - 仅添加 (Photos · Add Only)
仅在你主动点击「导出到相册」时使用，将草稿箱中的视频写入系统相册。不会读取你已有的任何照片或视频。

本地数据

录制的视频文件以 .mov 格式保存在 App 的沙盒目录 (Documents/Recordings/)。这些文件只存在于你的设备上， CoFrame 卸载时所有文件会随之删除。你也可以在 App 内的草稿箱里 随时单条删除。

App 偏好设置（默认画质、基准线选择、语言偏好等）保存在 iOS 标准的 UserDefaults 中，仅本机使用。

未成年人

CoFrame 不针对 13 岁以下用户设计，也不收集他们的任何信息 （我们不收集任何用户的任何信息）。

政策更新

如果未来隐私政策有任何变化，会在本页面更新「最后更新」日期。 重大变更会在 App 内提示。

CoFrame Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-26

CoFrame collects nothing and uploads nothing. All recordings live only in the app's local sandbox (Drafts) on your device, and are added to Photos only when you explicitly choose to.

No Data Collection

CoFrame does not:

  • Upload any video, audio, or metadata to any server
  • Integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs
  • Require account registration; collect no names, emails, phone numbers, or device IDs
  • Read any existing photos, videos, contacts, or other system data

System Permissions Used

📷 Camera
For landscape + portrait video recording. Used only while the app is open; never accessed in the background.
🎙 Microphone
For capturing audio with your video. Used only while the app is open; never records in the background.
🖼 Photos · Add Only
Used only when you explicitly tap "Save to Photos" in the Drafts view, to write a video file into your Photos library. Never reads any of your existing photos or videos.

Local Data

Recorded videos are saved as .mov files in the app's sandbox directory (Documents/Recordings/). These files exist only on your device and are removed when you uninstall CoFrame. You can also delete drafts individually inside the app.

App preferences (default quality, guide line choice, language preference, etc.) are stored in iOS's standard UserDefaults, accessible only locally.

Children

CoFrame is not directed at children under 13, and we collect no information from anyone (or from anyone of any age — we collect nothing).

Policy Updates

Any future change to this policy will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Material changes will also be surfaced in-app.
