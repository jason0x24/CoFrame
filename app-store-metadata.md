适合需要把内容同时发布到 YouTube、B站、视频号、抖音、小红书、Instagram Reels、TikTok 等多个平台的内容创作者。一次拍摄，省下重剪 / 重录的时间。

### Description (≤ 4000 chars)

```
CoFrame is a dual-format video recorder built for knowledge creators, educators, and reviewers.

▸ One shot, two formats
Tap record and CoFrame writes both a 16:9 landscape and a 9:16 portrait MOV from the same moment, in parallel. No re-cropping, no re-takes, no second camera rig.

▸ Live preview of both viewfinders
The main view shows your full landscape composition; a floating PiP in the corner shows the live portrait crop. Drag the PiP anywhere on screen and pinch to resize.

▸ Pannable portrait crop
Drag the dashed 9:16 indicator left or right to choose exactly which slice of the landscape becomes the portrait — left, center, or right edge. The PiP and the recorder both update in real time.

▸ iPhone Camera-grade controls
• Tap to focus, long-press for AE/AF lock, ±2 EV exposure bias
• Multi-lens zoom 0.5× / 1× / 2× / 5× (device-dependent) + pinch to zoom
• Rule of thirds, crosshair, and digital level composition guides
• 1080p30, 4K30, and 4K60 quality presets
• Front/back camera switch, torch, automatic HDR

▸ Sandbox drafts
Recordings are not auto-saved to Photos. They stay in the app's local drafts where you can review the landscape and portrait files separately, then choose which to export — or delete.

▸ Privacy first
All recordings live only on your device. The app uploads nothing, analyzes nothing, and collects nothing. No third-party SDKs. The Photos add-only permission is requested only when you explicitly tap "Save to Photos" — and never reads your existing photos.

▸ Bilingual
Follows your device language automatically, or set Chinese/English manually in Settings.

Made for creators who publish to YouTube, Bilibili, WeChat Channels, Douyin, Xiaohongshu, Instagram Reels, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts. Shoot once, save the re-edit time.

Requires: iPhone 12 or newer · iOS 26 or later
```

### 隐私政策网页内容建议

App 内的 `PrivacyPolicyView` 已经写好了文案，把它发布到一个公开网页就行。最简方案：

1. **GitHub Pages**：在 repo 里加个 `docs/privacy.html`，开启 Pages，URL 形如 `https://<user>.github.io/CoFrame/privacy.html`
2. **Notion 公开页面**：复制 `PrivacyPolicyView.swift` 里的文案到 Notion，发布为公开页
3. **Bear Blog / Telegraph**：免注册，几秒钟就能发布

## 截图 (Screenshots)

iOS App Store 要求至少**一组** 6.9" 或 6.7" 截图，**横屏方向**。建议每个语言区准备 3–6 张。

### 必需尺寸

| 设备 | 像素尺寸（横屏） | 替代 |
|---|---|---|
| 6.9" iPhone (17 Pro Max / 16 Pro Max) | 2868 × 1320 | 至少必填 |
| 6.5" iPhone (15 Plus 等) | 2778 × 1284 | 自动派生 |
| 6.1" iPhone (15 / 16 等) | 2556 × 1179 | 自动派生 |

### 推荐截图序列（5–6 张讲完产品故事）

1. **核心场景**：录制界面，主预览有人物，PiP 显示竖屏构图，红色录制按钮显眼
2. **可拖拽 PiP + 9:16 裁切框**：演示构图自由度
3. **多镜头变焦 + 控制 chip**：展示 0.5× / 1× / 2× / 5× pill + 顶部 chip 行
4. **草稿箱列表**：缩略图 + 横竖徽章 + 时长画质信息
5. **横竖切换播放 + 导出菜单**：展示导出选项弹框
6. **设置 + 隐私**（可选）：突出隐私卖点

### 截图采集流程

1. 用 iPhone 17 Pro Max 模拟器（Xcode → Window → Devices & Simulators）跑 app
2. Cmd+S 截屏（保存到桌面）
3. 用 Photoshop / Figma 加文案叠层（可选；纯截图也行）
4. 上传到 ASC 对应 size
